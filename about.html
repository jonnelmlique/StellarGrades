About Stellar Grades

Welcome to Stellar Grades, the ultimate platform for effortlessly calculating your Grade Weighted Average (GWA). Our mission is to empower students with a user-friendly tool that helps them track their academic performance and make informed decisions about their studies.

Stellar Grades is designed to make academic calculations transparent, accessible, and user-friendly. You can input your subjects, grades, and units to instantly calculate your GWA based on the selected rounding method. Whether you're a high school student or a university scholar, our tool is versatile and tailored to simplify the process of determining your academic standing.

Explore the various features of Stellar Grades, including the ability to add multiple subjects and manage your academic portfolio seamlessly. The tool also allows you to customize rounding preferences based on your academic institution's standards.

How to Use:

  1. Add your subjects along with corresponding grades and units.
  2. Select your rounding method (rounded or not rounded).
  3. Click "Calculate GWA" to get instant results.

Why Stellar Grades?

We believe in empowering students to excel academically by providing them with the tools they need. Our goal is to make academic calculations transparent, accessible, and user-friendly.
